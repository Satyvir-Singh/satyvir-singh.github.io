Industry Profile

I am a multiphysics simulation engineer with 13+ years of experience in CFD, thermal and heat-transfer analysis, and high-performance computing, working across continuum and rarefied flow regimes. I build and validate high-fidelity solvers using ANSYS Fluent, OpenFOAM, and in-house Discontinuous Galerkin (DG) codes, and deploy them on HPC clusters (MPI/SLURM) for large-scale parametric studies.

In recent years I have expanded into scientific machine learning — developing Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs), neural operators (FNO, DeepONet), and data-driven closure models using PyTorch that accelerate or replace costly traditional solvers. I am experienced in Python, C++, Fortran, and the full simulation pre/post-processing stack (ParaView, Tecplot, Gmsh, HDF5).

I am now actively seeking an industry position where I can apply my expertise in numerical simulation and machine learning to support engineering design, process optimization, and digital twin development. I am based in Aachen and hold a German Permanent Residence permit, making me fully available for long-term roles.

Research Background

I am currently a Scientific Staff / Postdoctoral Researcher at the Institute for Applied and Computational Mathematics, RWTH Aachen University, in the group of Prof. Manuel Torrilhon. My academic research sits at the intersection of scientific computing, computational fluid dynamics, and kinetic theory — designing high-order numerical methods that explain how shocks and instabilities propagate through complex flows.

I develop DG solvers for compressible multicomponent and rarefied gas flows, study Richtmyer–Meshkov and other hydrodynamic instabilities, and explore how ML and PINNs can extract closures and accelerate simulations of strongly non-equilibrium flows.

Before Aachen, I was a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (2018–2022) and earned my PhD at Gyeongsang National University, South Korea (2018), with prior M.Tech training at IIT Madras. I have authored 65+ peer-reviewed papers, edited three books, and serve as Guest Editor and Editorial Board member for several journals.
